Nilüfer Yanya’s latest album served as the basis for a graphic reinterpretation. Building on the album’s visual and atmospheric language, a unique design concept was developed. To complement this, an interactive music player was programmed to bring the album’s content to life digitally, seamlessly blending design and function.
