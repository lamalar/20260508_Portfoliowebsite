A campaign in collaboration with Club Südpol in Lucerne, to draw attention to the decline of clubs. The campaign aims to reflect the magic and diversity of a night out at a club and encourage people to go to clubs more often, thereby reigniting club culture.
The campaign was developed in a group project with Hannah Bucher, Julian Steiner, Lara Silberschmid, and myself.

1st BA Graphic Design, 2025
Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate, Screenprinting, Rispgraphy
